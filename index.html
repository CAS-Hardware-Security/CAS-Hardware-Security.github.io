About

  • Dr. Rui Hou is a professor at Institute of Information Engineering, CAS. His major research interest includes CPU architecture and security, as well as data center server design.
  • Before joined IIE, he was a processor of Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He published over 30 papers in international conferences and journals, and got more than 50 patents. Rui has also served more than ten times on conference organizing committees in international academia community. He received his bachelor and master degree in computer science from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2003, and earned his Ph.D in computer science from the Institute of Computing Technology of the CAS in 2007.
